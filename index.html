Core Philosophy: Why This Path

Dollar-cost averaging removes emotion. $10/day means you buy high sometimes, low sometimes, and over a long horizon your average cost smooths out. Don't try to time anything.
Self-custody is non-negotiable. Exchanges go bankrupt (FTX, Celsius, BlockFi). If you don't hold the keys, you don't hold the Bitcoin. Period.
Estate planning is the unsexy multiplier. Building wealth means nothing if your family can't access it or gets destroyed by probate and taxes. This is the step most people skip.
USD isn't "safe," it's just familiar. The dollar has lost ~97% of purchasing power since 1913. Bitcoin's fixed supply of 21M is the thesis. But concentration risk is real, so a small hedge in other hard assets is prudent risk management.
Never sell under pressure. Most wealth destruction comes from panic selling during drawdowns. If your time horizon is decades, a 50% drawdown is noise. The system below is built to make you boring and consistent.
Security is your #1 job. The biggest risk to your Bitcoin isn't the market. It's phishing, social engineering, bad seed phrase storage, and single points of failure. Treat this like protecting a vault.
